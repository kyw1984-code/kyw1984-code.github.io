Privacy Policy

Habitia · Last updated: April 30, 2026

Habitia ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored locally on your device only:

  • Habit names, schedules, and check-in history
  • App settings and preferences (theme, notifications)

We do not require an account. No personally identifiable information is collected or transmitted by Habitia itself.

2. Third-Party Services

2a. Google AdMob (free tier users)

If you are on the free plan, Habitia displays ads powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (for ad frequency capping)
  • General location (country/region)
  • App interaction data (ad views, clicks)

Habitia requests non-personalized ads only, which limits data collection. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Ads are not shown to Pro subscribers.

2b. RevenueCat (subscription management)

Habitia uses RevenueCat to process and manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Anonymous device identifiers

RevenueCat does not receive your habit data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

3. Notifications

If you grant permission, Habitia sends local push notifications as habit reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties beyond the services described above.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All habit data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. To delete subscription records, contact us at the address below.

6. Children's Privacy

Habitia is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
