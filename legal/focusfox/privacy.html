Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

FocusFox collects limited data to operate and improve the app:

  • Usage analytics — session counts, feature interactions (via Firebase Analytics, anonymized)
  • Crash reports — stack traces when the app crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics, no personal data)
  • Ad interactions — ad impressions and clicks (via Google AdMob)
  • Device info — OS version, device model (for crash diagnosis only)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

✅ All focus sessions, task lists, and timer data are stored on your device only. We never upload or access your personal focus data.

  • No name, email, or account registration required
  • No location data
  • No contacts or calendar access

3. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to understand app stability. Data is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google AdMob

FocusFox shows ads via AdMob. AdMob may use device advertising identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out via your device's ad settings. See Google's Advertising Policy.

4. Notifications

FocusFox sends local notifications to alert you when focus sessions or breaks end. These are processed entirely on-device and no notification content is sent to our servers.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Analytics and crash data are retained for up to 90 days by Firebase, then automatically deleted. To request deletion of any data associated with your device, email kyw1984@gmail.com.

6. GDPR / CCPA / CPRA

We comply with applicable privacy regulations. EU/EEA users have rights to access, rectify, erase, and port their data. California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal data).

Contact: kyw1984@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

FocusFox is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version is always available at this URL. Significant changes will be noted in the app.
