Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage analytics — feature interactions, screen views (Firebase Analytics, anonymized)
  • Crash reports — stack traces (Firebase Crashlytics, no financial data)
  • Ad interactions — ad impressions and clicks (Google AdMob)
  • Device info — OS version, device model (crash diagnosis only)

2. Receipt & Financial Data

✅ All receipt data is stored locally on your device only. Receipt images, OCR text, amounts, categories, and any financial data are processed and stored on-device using SQLite. We never upload, transmit, or access your receipt data on any server.

  • Camera access is used only to scan receipts within the app
  • Photo library access is used to import receipt images
  • Exported CSV/PDF files are generated on-device and shared by you directly

3. Important Financial Disclaimer

⚠️ ReceiptVault is a personal organization tool only. It is not a certified accounting, tax, or financial software. Do not rely on ReceiptVault data for tax filings, audits, or any official financial reporting without independent verification. The developer is not liable for financial or tax consequences arising from the use of this app.

4. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google)

Analytics and crash reporting only. No receipt data reaches Firebase. Processed per Google's Privacy Policy.

Google AdMob

Ad delivery. May use device advertising identifiers. Opt out via device settings.

5. Permissions

  • Camera — to scan and photograph receipts
  • Photo Library — to import receipt images

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your receipt data stays on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app or use the in-app delete function. Analytics data is retained by Firebase for up to 90 days. To request deletion of analytics data: kyw1984@gmail.com

7. GDPR / CCPA / CPRA

We comply with applicable privacy laws. Contact us at kyw1984@gmail.com to exercise your rights.
